FAQ · Frequently Asked Questions
Is this a game to play?

This is not the full game. It helps you keep score while you play La Cuenta with the real cards.

Is it free to use?

Yes, it is completely free.

Is my data or scoring sent to someone?

No. Your scores stay on your own device and are not shared by this app.

Can I use it offline without internet?

Yes. Open it once with internet, then you can use it later without internet.

Can I install this as an app?

Yes. You can add it to your phone, tablet, or computer home screen and open it like a normal app.
